SECTION 1 — VPN ACCESS
============================================================
Page 3

1.1 VPN Setup
All remote employees must use the company VPN (GlobalProtect) to access internal
systems. VPN credentials are provisioned by IT during onboarding.

1.2 Supported Platforms
- Windows 10 / 11
- macOS 12 (Monterey) and above
- Ubuntu 22.04 LTS

1.3 Troubleshooting
If the VPN fails to connect, clear the cache via Settings > Clear Cache and retry.
Contact IT helpdesk at it-support@acmecorp.com for persistent issues.

SECTION 2 — PASSWORD POLICY
============================================================
Page 6

2.1 Requirements
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include:
- Uppercase and lowercase letters
- At least one number
- At least one special character (@, #, $, %, etc.)

2.2 Rotation
Passwords must be changed every 90 days. The last 10 passwords cannot be reused.

2.3 Multi-Factor Authentication
MFA is mandatory for all accounts. Approved MFA methods: Google Authenticator,
Microsoft Authenticator, or hardware TOTP keys.

============================================================
SECTION 3 — DATA CLASSIFICATION
============================================================
Page 10

3.1 Classification Levels
- Public: Marketing materials, press releases.
- Internal: General business documents.
- Confidential: Financial data, employee records, client contracts.
- Restricted: Trade secrets, source code, encryption keys.

3.2 Handling Rules
Confidential and Restricted data must be encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in
transit (TLS 1.3). Access is granted on a need-to-know basis.

SECTION 4 — INCIDENT RESPONSE
============================================================
Page 14

4.1 Reporting a Security Incident
All suspected security incidents must be reported immediately to security@acmecorp.com.
Do not attempt to investigate or remediate the incident on your own.

4.2 Incident Severity
- P1 (Critical): Active breach, data exfiltration. Response time: 1 hour.
- P2 (High): Suspected compromise. Response time: 4 hours.
- P3 (Medium): Policy violation. Response time: 1 business day.
